4. Initial Findings
- TF tends to highlight words that appear frequently across the corpus.
- TF-IDF tends to highlight words that are more distinctive or representative in specific reviews.
- If positive and negative groups show different keywords, this suggests that keyword extraction can capture sentiment-related patterns.
- The comparison between TF and TF-IDF helps reveal the difference between frequency-based importance and distinctiveness-based importance.
